Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a one-time payment or a subscription?

The plans listed are one-time payments for the specified access duration (e.g., 12 months for the PRO plan). Your access will expire at the end of the period, and you can choose to purchase a new plan at any time. There are no automatic recurring charges.

What do I need to use the AI Film Director System?

You only need a web browser to access our system. The script generation is done on our servers. To generate the final video, you will need access to Google's Veo video generation model and credits for it, which we provide a guide for.

What does "Unlimited Scripts" on the PRO plan mean?

It means you can generate as many complete, multi-scene video scripts as you need throughout your 12-month access period, subject to our fair use policy to prevent automated abuse. For 99.9% of creators and agencies, it's effectively unlimited.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Due to the nature of digital services and the costs associated with AI generation, we do not offer refunds. We encourage you to review our examples and features before making a purchase.
