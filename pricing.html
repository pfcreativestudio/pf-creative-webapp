Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a one-time payment or a subscription?

The plans listed are one-time payments for the specified access duration (e.g., 12 months for the PRO plan). Your access will expire at the end of the period, and you can choose to purchase a new plan at any time. There are no automatic recurring charges.

What do I need to use the AI Film Director System?

You only need a web browser to access our system. Project processing is done on our servers. To generate the final video, you will need access to the video generation model (e.g., Veo) and credits, which we provide documentation for.

What does "Unlimited Projects" on the PRO plan mean?

It means you can create as many complete, multi-scene projects as you need throughout your access period, subject to our fair use policy. For almost all creators it's effectively unlimited.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Due to the nature of digital services and the costs associated with AI generation, we do not offer refunds. We encourage you to review examples and features before making a purchase.
